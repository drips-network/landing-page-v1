A funding ecosystem

Projects drip funds to their 🗂 dependencies, fans drip funds to 💖 favorite developers. Together they create a sustainable ♻️ funding ecosystem.

How it works

1

A creator sets a goal 🏅 and a minimum fan contribution.

2

Fans drip funds in exchange for community 🎟 membership tokens.

3

Creators choose 💫 benefits their fans get, like Snapshot voting or Discord access.

4

Creators spread the love 💌 and drip a percentage of their income to communities or users.
