An 💎 Ethereum app for generating 📮 recurring income from 😍 fans to 🎨 creators. No predatory 👔 middle-men or 🏦 banks involved.

A funding ecosystem

Fans drip funds to their 💖 favorite developers, projects drip funds to their 🗂 dependencies. Together they create a circular and sustainable ♻️ funding ecosystem.

How it works

A creator sets a goal 🏅 and a minimum fan contribution.

Fans drip funds in exchange for community 🎟 membership tokens.

Creators choose 💫 benefits their fans get, like Snapshot voting or Discord access.

Creators spread the love 💌 and drip a percentage of their income to communities or users.
